Crystal Collector

There are 4 crystals below, by clicking you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by adding your total score to equal the random number generated, if your score goes above the random number you lose


The value of each crystal is hidden from you till you click on it.


Each time the game starts each crystal will be given a random value.
